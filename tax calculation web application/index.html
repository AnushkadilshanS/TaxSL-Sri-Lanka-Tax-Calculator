TaxSL: Sri Lanka Tax Calculator

Tax Rules & Regulations

  • No tax for annual income up to LKR 300,000.
  • 6% tax for income between LKR 300,001 - 500,000.
  • 12% tax for income between LKR 500,001 - 1,000,000.
  • 18% tax for income above LKR 1,000,000.
  • Ensure to include all taxable allowances.
